## What was wrong (the audit findings)

Full details in `tasks/PBV_INTAKE_AUDIT_2026-05-15.md`. Headline defects:

1. **Bootstrap endpoint never selected `intake_data`** — root cause of empty Review summary. `app/api/t/[token]/pbv-full-app/route.ts:31-33`.
2. **Header reads "Section 8 of 7" at Review step** — off-by-one in `app/pbv-full-app/[token]/intake/[section]/page.tsx:81-142` + `components/pbv/intake/IntakeShell.tsx:80-91`.
3. **Dashboard "Upload required documents → Start" → 404** — route `app/pbv-full-app/[token]/documents/page.tsx` doesn't exist.
4. **Dashboard "Review and sign your summary → Start" → 404** — un-tokenized redirect in `app/pbv-full-app/[token]/sign/summary/page.tsx:55`.
5. **`buildSummary` emits only a one-line tagline per section** — most captured data hidden.
6. **Tenants cannot view, preview, or download uploaded documents** — `TenantDocumentUpload.tsx` has no view UI, `Document` interface has no `file_url`.
7. (Inferred) **`useSectionVisibility` derives age as `m.is_minor ? 10 : 30`** — medical section never appears for elderly applicants.
8. (Inferred) **Edit-from-summary may lose last <600ms of typing** — no flush before navigation.

Pattern: prior audits read components in isolation and declared green without exercising the data path or clicking the app. The bug is the data path; the components were "correct" on their own.

## Critical updates from Windsurf sweep on PRD-35

Windsurf did a codebase sweep before starting PRD-35 and found:

**The actual storage bucket** for `application_documents` is `form-submissions`, not `submissions`. Every upload route writes there:
- tenant doc upload (`pbv-full-app/documents/[id]/upload`)
- staff doc upload, intake commit, staff submission docs

**Ripple into PRD-33 F6:** the tenant View button originally hardcoded `submissions` per the original PRD-35 framing. Fixed in PRD-33 and its prompt this session — now hardcodes `form-submissions`. PRD-35 will swap to a resolver call.

**New defect found in the sweep (also fixed by PRD-35):** Stanton-side `DocumentViewer` calls `/api/admin/submissions/${doc.id}/documents/${doc.id}/signed-url` — that route does not exist. **Stanton staff have not been able to view documents from the review surface at all.** Silently broken. PRD-35's F2 creates the missing endpoint. Build report should explicitly call this out as a pre-existing defect, not a routine refactor.

**HACH viewer separate question:** `app/api/hach/documents/[id]/signed-url/route.ts` hardcodes `pbv-applications`. May be legitimate (stamped HACH packets in a separate bucket from raw uploads) or another silent bug. Flagged for a separate ticket — NOT in PRD-35 scope.

**F5 of PRD-35 (storage_bucket column) is unnecessary** per the sweep — bucket-by-doc_type is reliable.
